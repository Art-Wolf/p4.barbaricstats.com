Mafia

Description

Implement a multiplayer game where people can chat in a game lobby before pairing off into a game. The game revolves around two phases, day and night. Every player can talk during the day and only the mob can talk privatly at night. During the night the mob kill off a player. During the day all players vote on a player to lynch.
